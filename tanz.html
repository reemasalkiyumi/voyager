ARUSHA, TANZANIA

Arusha

Arusha is known as the gateway to world-famous national parks such as the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Lake Manyara, and Mount Kilimanjaro. It offers safari adventures, rich culture, wildlife experiences, and beautiful scenery.

Arusha offers delicious African cuisine including pilau, nyama choma, ugali, freshly grilled meats, and tropical fruits.

Highlights include Arusha National Park, Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Mount Meru, and incredible wildlife reserves.

Is Arusha Good For…?

  • Solo Travelers: Group safaris, local guides, and friendly atmosphere.
  • Families: Safe lodges, wildlife parks, and educational adventure experiences.
  • Couples: Luxury safari camps, romantic sunsets, and private game drives.
  • Adventure Seekers: Hiking, climbing Mount Meru, hot-air balloon safaris, and wildlife viewing.
  • Food Lovers: Grilled meats, spiced pilau rice, street snacks, and fresh produce.
Weather

Mild weather year-round. Best months: June–October.

Average Trip Cost

Budget: $70–120/day · Luxury safari: $300+ per day.

Best For

Wildlife, nature, adventure, and unforgettable safaris.
