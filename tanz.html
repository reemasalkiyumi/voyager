CITY PROFILE

Arusha

Arusha, located in northern Tanzania, is the gateway to safari adventures in Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, and Mount Kilimanjaro. Surrounded by lush landscapes, national parks, and wildlife, it offers visitors a unique blend of nature, culture, and thrilling safari experiences.
