CITY PROFILE

Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is known as the "Eternal City" for its rich history that spans over 2,500 years. It is home to world-famous landmarks such as the Colosseum, Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, and St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City. Rome played a central role in the Roman Empire and Renaissance, influencing art, architecture, law, and culture worldwide.
