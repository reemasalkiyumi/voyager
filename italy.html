ROME

Rome

Rome, Italy’s capital, is a museum of ancient history in the open air — with the Colosseum, Roman Forum, Vatican City, piazzas, fountains, and world-famous cuisine that attracts visitors from all over the world.

Rome is famous for pizza, pasta, gelato, espresso, street food, and traditional trattorias.

Top landmarks include the Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Pantheon, Trevi Fountain, Spanish Steps, and ancient ruins.

Is Rome Good For…?

  • Solo Travelers: Safe, walkable, lots of tours, cafés & museums.
  • Families: Parks, gelato, museums, piazzas & easy transport.
  • Couples: Romantic views, fine dining, sunset spots.
  • Adventure Seekers: Urban exploration, biking, guided history tours.
  • Food Lovers: Pasta, pizza, desserts, wine tasting and markets.
Weather

Mediterranean climate. Best months: April–June & September–October.

Average Trip Cost

Budget: €50–80/day · Luxury: €200+ per day.

Best For

History, culture, food, romance & world-famous sightseeing.
